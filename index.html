Personal Description

I am an undergraduate in the Department of Computer Science at Princeton University. My current research interests center around natural language processing and building agents that can reason about their enviornments to make informed decisions. Previously, I have published some work in deep learning theory and computer vision. On the side, I am minoring in philosophy, where I am interested in questions of sentience and consciousness, especially as they pertain to AI.
